# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is IriSync?

IriSync is a comprehensive social media management platform that combines AI-powered content creation with unified management of multiple social media channels. It offers content scheduling, analytics, and team collaboration features through a tiered subscription model.

### What platforms does IriSync support?

IriSync supports the following platforms:
- Facebook
- Instagram (and Threads)
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Pinterest
- Reddit
- Mastodon

### What subscription plans do you offer?

We offer three subscription tiers:

1. **Creator** ($80/month): Ideal for individual content creators with up to 5 social accounts.
2. **Influencer** ($200/month): Perfect for growing influencers with unlimited social accounts and additional features.
3. **Enterprise** ($250/seat, minimum 5 seats, $150/additional seat): Designed for teams and businesses that need advanced collaboration and custom features.

## AI Features

### How does the AI content generation work?

IriSync uses advanced AI models to help you create content for various platforms. Our AI can generate post ideas, write captions, suggest hashtags, and even analyze the potential performance of your content. The AI adapts to each platform's specific requirements and can match your brand voice.

### Are there limits to AI usage?

Yes, AI feature usage is based on a token system:
- Creator tier: 100 tokens/month
- Influencer tier: 500 tokens/month
- Enterprise tier: 5,000 base tokens/month (1,000 tokens per user for first 5 seats) + 500 tokens per additional seat

### Can I purchase additional AI tokens?

Yes, you can purchase additional AI tokens if you need more than your subscription includes. Additional token packages are available at preferred rates for subscribers.

## Account Management

### How do I connect my social media accounts?

To connect your social accounts:
1. Go to the Dashboard and click on "Settings"
2. Select the "Connections" tab
3. Click "Connect" next to the platform you want to add
4. Follow the authentication steps for that platform
5. Once connected, the account will appear in your dashboard

### Is there a limit to how many social accounts I can connect?

This depends on your subscription:
- Creator tier: Limited to 5 social accounts
- Influencer and Enterprise tiers: Unlimited social accounts

### Can I transfer my subscription to someone else?

Subscriptions are tied to your account and cannot be transferred. If you need to change the account owner, please contact our support team for assistance.

## Content Management

### Can I schedule posts across multiple platforms at once?

Yes, you can create a post and schedule it to be published on multiple platforms simultaneously. You can also customize the content for each platform before scheduling.

### Does IriSync support video content?

Yes, IriSync supports video content scheduling and publishing for all supported platforms. Video scheduling is available on the Influencer and Enterprise tiers.

### Can I bulk upload and schedule content?

Yes, our Influencer and Enterprise tiers support bulk scheduling, allowing you to upload and schedule multiple posts at once using our calendar interface or CSV upload feature.

## Troubleshooting

### Why didn't my scheduled post publish?

There could be several reasons:
1. Check if the platform experienced API issues (see our status page)
2. Verify that your account connection is still valid
3. Check if the content violated platform policies
4. Confirm that you didn't exceed posting limitations for that platform

If you need help, please contact our support team with the post ID and scheduled time.

### How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link to create a new password

### Why am I seeing "Connection Error" for my social accounts?

This typically happens when the authorization for your social account has expired. To fix this:
1. Go to Settings > Connections
2. Find the account with the error
3. Click "Reconnect" and follow the authentication steps
4. If the problem persists, try removing the account and adding it again

If you continue to experience issues, please contact our support team.
